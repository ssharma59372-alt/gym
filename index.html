About Us

Welcome to Unique Fitness Point

Founded in 2010, Unique Fitness Point has been helping people achieve their fitness goals for over a decade. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and motivating environment for people of all fitness levels.

We believe that fitness is not just about physical appearance, but about overall health and wellbeing. Our certified trainers are dedicated to helping you create a sustainable fitness routine that fits your lifestyle.

With over 10,000 square feet of premium workout space, we offer the latest equipment, diverse classes, and personalized training programs to help you succeed.

Our Services

Strength Training

Access to premium strength training equipment including free weights, machines, and functional training areas with guidance from expert trainers.

Cardio Zone

State-of-the-art cardio equipment including treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, and rowing machines with personalized workout programs.

Group Classes

Diverse group fitness classes including yoga, spin, HIIT, Zumba, and pilates led by certified instructors in our dedicated studio spaces.
